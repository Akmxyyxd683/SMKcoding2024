The VALORANT encyclopedia that anyone can edit! The definitive wiki resource for Riot Games' competitive 5v5 character-based tactical shooter, VALORANT, that was released on June 2nd, 2020. Set in a near-future Earth, you team up with 4 other players against 5 enemies in round-based combat with an agent of your choice. Creativity is your greatest weapon.
